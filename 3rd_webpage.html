Apple and its high quality philosophy



Apple is one of the most known brands, is one of the most valuable brands on the

stock market, and is one of the most influential brands in our modern time. But

how did Apple achieve these tremendous successes?

Apple is different from other brands. If you think about Dell, HP, or Lenovo, what picture

will be in your mind if you think about their computers? Plastic! Black plastic.

A MacBook feels very valuable because of its chassis, a Lenovo ThinkPad just feels

cheap. In fact, if you push with your finger on the material between the keyboards of

the laptop, a plastic laptop extremely bands and you just think how cheap it is.

A MacBook can withstand this pressure and only bands slightly. Apple uses valuable materials

and well-developed assembly processes so that people believe and feel how good

their MacBook or iPhone is. Steve Jobs once said that Apple does not want to sell

trash. Yes, I think Lenovo, HP, or Dell want to sell trash because they want to sell as many as

possible products to gain revenue. Apple wants to sell the best products, not

the most. Apple is a valued brand because of its focus on high quality.
